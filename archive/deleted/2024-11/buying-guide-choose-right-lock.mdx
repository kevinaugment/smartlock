## Compatibility Checklist

✅ Door thickness: 1-3/8" to 2-1/4"
✅ Deadbolt hole: Standard 2-1/8"
✅ Backset: 2-3/4" or 2-3/8"
✅ Door type: Wood or metal
✅ WiFi signal strength at door
