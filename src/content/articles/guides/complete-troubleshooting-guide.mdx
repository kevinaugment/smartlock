### Symptom: Intermittent Connection (Works Sometimes)

**What You See**:
- Lock connects successfully 50-80% of attempts
- Other times: "Connection timeout" or very slow  - 30+ seconds
- Pattern: Worse at certain times of day OR after specific events

**Root Causes**:

| Cause | Symptoms | Solution | Success Rate |
|-------|----------|----------|--------------|
| **Weak signal** | Worse when farther from hub/router | Move hub closer, add repeater | 90% |
| **2.4GHz interference** | Worse 6-9pm (neighbors home using WiFi), microwave running | Change WiFi channel, use 5GHz for other devices | 70% |
| **Battery low** | Worse when battery <30% | Replace batteries | 100% |
| **Loose battery contact** | Pattern: Works after jiggling lock/door | Clean battery contacts, bend contact springs slightly | 85% |
| **Firmware bug** | Started after firmware update | Rollback firmware OR wait for fix | 60% |
